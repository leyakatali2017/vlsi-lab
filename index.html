VLSI System Design

Very-large-scale integration (VLSI) is the way of creating an integrated circuit (IC) by combining millions of transistors into a single chip which is based on miniaturization of microchip used in present day electronic hardwae, medical instrument etc. Design and realization of VLSI systems using next generation VLSI technologies require alliance between scientists and engineers in the areas of system architecture, logic/circuit design, chip/wafer fabrication, packaging, testing etc.In this Generation of noble design, specifications and verification are performed at all levels of abstraction which includes system, logic, circuit, transistor, and process levels.

Job Oriented VLSI Courses

Our job-oriented VLSI courses have been designed carefully based on the industry requirements. These advanced VLSI courses train the electronics engineers extensively on both the design and verification methodologies like RTL design and UVM methodologies and make them specialized in the advanced VLSI technology domains like Design For Test, Low Power Verification, Analog Mixed Signal Verification, etc. Also our engineers work on multiple industry standard projects which use the SoC protocols and gain real time project experience through our internship program.

VLSI Internship

VLSI Internship program is basically for the electronics engineers who are looking for an experience on the real time VLSI projects, while pursuing the engineering course. As part of this internship program the engineering graduates will learn the VLSI Design flow and methodologies, VLSI Verification Flow and Verification methodologies, RTL design, Digital Electronics, Verilog HDL and Systemverilog for Verification and do the real time project independently.
