Highly Qualified Faculties

Teachers are well qualified, knowledgeable and extremely helpful. Many teachers in the our deparmet are well qualified with PhDs and industry based research work. Teachers provide extra classes to students who find trouble with the concept.


Real Time VLSI Projects

Very-Large-Scale-Integration(VLSI) is the process of creating an integrated circuit (IC) by combining thousands of transistors into a single chip. We offer VLSI projects that can be applied in real time solutions by optimization of processors thereby increasing the effieciency of many systems.


Career Opportunities & Completion Certificate

VLSI field is highly technical and completely based on electronics engineering. Computer Science / IT Engineers can also try for some specific jobs, with good knowledge of digital fundamentals and software programming. There are plenty of job opportunities in the Semiconductor industry.
