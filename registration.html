VLSI 2016 Registration

Registration fee for the workshop is as follows:
    1) Students: 500 INR
    2) Others: 1500 INR

Payment of registration fees is to be made through DD, in favor of Finance Officer, JNU and payable at Delhi. Participants are required to fill the registration form below and provide details of the DD. Original DD should be submitted at the workshop venue.

For registered members, on spot payment in cash will also be allowed based on first come first served basis depending on the availability of seats. Participants are required to simply fill the registration form below without the DD details.

Please note that the number of seats are limited and will be allocated on first come first serve basis.
